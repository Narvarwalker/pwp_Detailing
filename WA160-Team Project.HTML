We Come To You

Power Washing Pro's is a fully mobile auto detailing service. We bring everything required to detail your vehicle at your home or place of business for a seamless and convenient customer service experience. We proudly serve Nassau, Suffolk, NYC and surrounding areas.


We Don't Scratch

Your local drive-through car wash carries dirt and debris that will scratch your paint, leaving behind swirls and lines that are easily visible in direct sunlight. WALKER POWER WASHING PRO'S uses only the most gentle micro-fiber cloths to help prolong the life of your paint

Cleaner Is Safer

Our detailing methods and products are effective at stopping the spread of germs and other potentially dangerous pathogens such as COVID-19. We abide by all CDC Guidelines and understand the importance of keeping both our employees and our customers safe


Fully Insured

We're serious about caring for your vehicle. That's why we're ready to show proof of insurance at any point during your service. Whether we're working in your garage or in your driveway, you can rest assured -- Your car is always safe with us.


"Greener is Better"

As a commitment to our customers and the environment, we strive to minimize water waste (up to 90% less than your local car wash) and use eco-friendly products when possible. We protect your lawn and the environment, without sacrificing the integrity of your vehicle.
